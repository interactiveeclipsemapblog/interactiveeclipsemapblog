Introduction

If you are a new student or visitor to Lasell College, you may need help navigating the campus. Fortunately, Lasell College has an interactive map that can guide you through the campus. In this article, we will review the Lasell College Campus Map and give you some tips on how to use it.

What is Lasell College Campus Map?

Lasell College Campus Map is a web-based tool that shows the location of buildings, parking, athletic facilities, and other campus amenities. It is designed to make it easier for students, faculty, staff, and visitors to get around campus.

How to Access Lasell College Campus Map

You can access Lasell College Campus Map by visiting the official website of Lasell College. Click on the “Campus Map” link from the top menu to open the map. Alternatively, you can search for “Lasell College Campus Map” on Google and click on the first link that appears in the search results.

How to Use Lasell College Campus Map

Once you have opened the Lasell College Campus Map, you will see a detailed view of the campus. To navigate the map, you can use the zoom in and zoom out buttons to adjust the view. You can also drag the map to view different parts of the campus. The map is interactive, so you can click on any building or amenity to get more information.

Features of Lasell College Campus Map

The Lasell College Campus Map has several features that can help you navigate the campus. Some of the key features include:

Building and Amenity Search

You can search for buildings and amenities on the Lasell College Campus Map by using the search bar. Simply type in the name of the building or amenity you are looking for, and the map will show you its location.

Indoor Maps

The Lasell College Campus Map also has indoor maps of some buildings on campus. This feature can be especially helpful if you are trying to find your way around a large building or if you are looking for a specific room.

Directions

The Lasell College Campus Map also has a directions feature that can generate walking or driving directions from one location to another on campus. This feature can be helpful if you are trying to get from one building to another quickly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Lasell College Campus Map available on mobile?

A: Yes, Lasell College Campus Map is available on mobile devices. You can access the map from your smartphone or tablet by visiting the official website of Lasell College.

Q: Can I print a copy of the Lasell College Campus Map?

Q: What if I can’t find the building or amenity I am looking for?

The Lasell College Campus Map is a helpful tool for navigating the campus. With its search and directions features, you can easily find your way around the campus. Whether you are a new student or a visitor, the Lasell College Campus Map can make your visit to Lasell College more enjoyable.
