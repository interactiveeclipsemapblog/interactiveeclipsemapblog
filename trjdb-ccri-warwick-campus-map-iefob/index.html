Ccri Warwick Campus Map: A Complete Guide

admin January 26, 2023 No Comments

Introduction

Are you a new student at the Community College of Rhode Island (CCRI) Warwick campus? Or maybe you’re a returning student looking for a refresher on the campus layout. Either way, you’ve come to the right place. In this article, we will be providing you with a complete guide to the CCRI Warwick campus map.

History

The CCRI Warwick campus was established in 1969, and since then has grown to become one of the largest community college campuses in Rhode Island. The campus is located in Warwick, Rhode Island, and serves students from all over the state.

Campus Map

The CCRI Warwick campus map can be found on the CCRI website or by searching for it on Google. The map is divided into different sections, each representing a different building on campus. The main buildings on campus include the Knight Campus, the Liston Campus, and the Flanagan Campus.

The Knight Campus

The Knight Campus is the largest building on the CCRI Warwick campus. It houses the majority of the classrooms, labs, and offices. The building is divided into different wings, each representing a different department or program. The Knight Campus also has a cafeteria, a bookstore, and a student lounge.

The Liston Campus

The Liston Campus is located across the street from the Knight Campus. It houses the nursing program, as well as other health science programs. The Liston Campus also has a student lounge and a fitness center.

The Flanagan Campus

The Flanagan Campus is located in Lincoln, Rhode Island, which is about a 20-minute drive from the Warwick campus. The Flanagan Campus houses the culinary arts program and the automotive technology program.

FAQs

Where can I park on campus?

There are several parking lots on the CCRI Warwick campus. The largest parking lot is located next to the Knight Campus, and there are also smaller parking lots located near the Liston Campus and the Flanagan Campus.

How do I get to the Knight Campus?

The Knight Campus is located at 400 East Avenue in Warwick, Rhode Island. If you are coming from the north or south, take exit 10 off of I-95 and follow signs for CCRI. If you are coming from the east or west, take Route 4 to exit 8 and follow signs for CCRI.

What services are available to students on campus?

CCRI offers a variety of services to students, including tutoring, counseling, and disability services. The campus also has a health center and a career services center.

Conclusion

We hope this guide to the CCRI Warwick campus map has been helpful. Remember to take advantage of all the resources available to you as a CCRI student, and don’t be afraid to ask for help if you need it. Good luck on your academic journey!
