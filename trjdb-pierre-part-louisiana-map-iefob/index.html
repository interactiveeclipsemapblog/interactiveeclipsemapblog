Where is Pierre Part Located?

Pierre Part is a small town situated in the southeastern part of Louisiana. It is part of Assumption Parish and is located along the eastern bank of the Atchafalaya River. The town is approximately 85 miles west of New Orleans and 30 miles southeast of Baton Rouge.
