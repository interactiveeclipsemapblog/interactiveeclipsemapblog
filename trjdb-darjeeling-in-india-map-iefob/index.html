Darjeeling In India Map: Exploring The Enchanting Hill Station

admin December 4, 2022 No Comments

Introduction

Darjeeling, located in the Indian state of West Bengal, is a beautiful hill station that attracts tourists from all over the world. With its picturesque landscapes, tea plantations, and the famous Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, this place is a must-visit destination for anyone who loves nature and adventure. In this article, we will explore the Darjeeling map and discover the hidden gems of this enchanting hill station.

Where is Darjeeling Located?

Darjeeling is located in the northern part of West Bengal, bordering Nepal and Bhutan. It is situated at an altitude of 6,710 feet above sea level and covers an area of 10.57 square miles. The town is surrounded by beautiful mountains and is known for its cool climate and natural beauty.

What are the Main Attractions of Darjeeling?

Darjeeling has plenty of attractions to offer to its visitors. Some of the most popular ones include the Tiger Hill, which is famous for its sunrise view, the Batasia Loop, a spiral railway track offering panoramic views of the town, and the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Other noteworthy attractions include the Padmaja Naidu Himalayan Zoological Park, the Peace Pagoda, and the Happy Valley Tea Estate.

How to Reach Darjeeling?

The nearest airport to Darjeeling is Bagdogra Airport, which is located at a distance of 68 km. From there, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach Darjeeling. Alternatively, you can also take the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway from New Jalpaiguri, which is a unique experience in itself.

Exploring the Darjeeling Map

Now that we have an idea of where Darjeeling is located and what are its main attractions, let’s explore the Darjeeling map in detail.

Chowrasta

Chowrasta is the heart of Darjeeling town and is a popular tourist spot. It is a flat open area that offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains. You can spend some time here relaxing, sipping tea or coffee, and watching the world go by.

Mall Road

Mall Road is a pedestrian-only street that runs through the heart of Darjeeling. It is lined with shops, restaurants, and cafes and is a great place to shop for souvenirs or enjoy a meal.

Ghoom Monastery

Ghoom Monastery is one of the oldest Tibetan Buddhist monasteries in Darjeeling. It is located at a distance of 7 km from the town center and offers a peaceful and serene environment.

Tiger Hill

Tiger Hill is located at a distance of 11 km from Darjeeling town and is famous for its sunrise view. You can reach here early in the morning to witness the sun rising over the Kanchenjunga mountain range.

Batasia Loop

Batasia Loop is a spiral railway track located at a distance of 5 km from Darjeeling town. It offers panoramic views of the town and is a great place to enjoy a cup of tea while watching the toy train pass through the loop.

Conclusion

Darjeeling is a beautiful hill station that offers a perfect blend of nature, adventure, and culture. With its stunning landscapes, tea plantations, and the famous Darjeeling Himalayan Railway, this place is a must-visit destination for anyone who loves to explore. We hope this article has given you a good idea of what to expect in Darjeeling and has helped you plan your trip better.
