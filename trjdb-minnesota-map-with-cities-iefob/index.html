It is home to many cultural institutions, including the Walker Art Center, the Minneapolis Institute of Art, and the Guthrie Theater. St. Paul: St. Paul is the state capital of Minnesota and is known for its historic architecture, museums, and restaurants. It is home to the Minnesota State Capitol, the Science Museum of Minnesota, and the Cathedral of Saint Paul. Duluth: Duluth is a port city located on Lake Superior and is known for its scenic beauty and outdoor activities. It is home to the Great Lakes Aquarium, the Aerial Lift Bridge, and the Canal Park district. Rochester: Rochester is a city located in southeastern Minnesota and is known for the Mayo Clinic, one of the largest medical centers in the world. It is also home to the Rochester Art Center and the Quarry Hill Nature Center.
