The World of Code Geass

In the world of Code Geass, the year is 2023 and the world is divided into three superpowers: the Holy Britannian Empire, the Chinese Federation, and the European Union. The story of Code Geass follows the adventures of Lelouch vi Britannia, a former prince of the Holy Britannian Empire who becomes a freedom fighter in order to take down the oppressive regime of his own father.

The Holy Britannian Empire

The Holy Britannian Empire is the largest and most powerful of the three superpowers in the world of Code Geass. It controls most of the Americas, as well as parts of Europe, Africa, and Asia. The empire is ruled by Emperor Charles zi Britannia, Lelouch’s father, who is a ruthless and tyrannical leader. The empire is known for its use of giant humanoid mechs called Knightmare Frames, which are used both in combat and for transportation.

The Britannian Homeland

The Britannian Homeland is the heart of the Holy Britannian Empire, and it is where the royal family resides. It is located in what was once known as the United Kingdom, and it is the most heavily fortified location in the world of Code Geass. The Homeland is protected by a massive network of defenses, including a fleet of airships, a series of underground tunnels, and a powerful shield system that can deflect most attacks.

The Area 11 (Japan)

The Area 11, formerly known as Japan, is a conquered territory of the Holy Britannian Empire. It is heavily oppressed by the Britannians, who have taken away the Japanese people’s rights and forced them to work in mines and factories. Lelouch, who was once a prince of the Britannian Empire, becomes a freedom fighter in the Area 11 in order to help the Japanese people regain their independence.

The Chinese Federation

The Chinese Federation is the second largest of the three superpowers in the world of Code Geass. It controls most of Asia, including China, Korea, and parts of Southeast Asia. The federation is ruled by a council of high-ranking officials, and it is known for its advanced technology and powerful military.

The Federation Capital

The Federation Capital is located in what was once known as Beijing, China. It is a sprawling metropolis that is known for its advanced technology and towering skyscrapers. The capital is heavily guarded and protected by a powerful military force, which includes giant mechs similar to the Knightmare Frames used by the Holy Britannian Empire.

The European Union

The European Union is the smallest of the three superpowers in the world of Code Geass. It controls most of Europe, including France, Germany, and Italy. The Union is ruled by a council of representatives from each of its member states, and it is known for its cultural diversity and progressive policies.

The Union Capital

The Union Capital is located in what was once known as Brussels, Belgium. It is a cosmopolitan city that is known for its architecture, art, and fashion. The capital is heavily guarded and protected by a sophisticated security system, which includes drones and other advanced technologies.
