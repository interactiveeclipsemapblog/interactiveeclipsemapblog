What is a US Map Vector?

A US map vector is a digital file that contains a scalable and editable map of the United States. Unlike raster images, which are made up of pixels, vector maps are made up of mathematical equations that can be resized without losing quality. This means that you can use a US map vector for any project, from small icons to large billboards, without worrying about pixelation or blurriness.
