    Introduction

    If you are a new student at Suny Brockport, one of the first things you’ll need to do is familiarize yourself with the campus map. This map will help you navigate your way around the campus and find your classes, residence halls, and other important locations.

    Where to Find the Campus Map

    The Suny Brockport campus map is available on the school’s website. You can also pick up a physical copy of the map at the information desk in the Seymour College Union.

    Understanding the Map

    The campus map is divided into several sections, including academic buildings, administrative buildings, residence halls, and recreational facilities. Each section is color-coded to make it easier to find what you are looking for. Additionally, each building is labeled with its name and number.

    Using the Map

    To use the campus map, start by locating the building you need to find. Look for the building’s name and number on the map, and then find it on the corresponding section of the map. You can also use the map to plan your route from one location to another.

    Common Locations on Campus

    Some of the most common locations on campus include the Seymour College Union, Drake Memorial Library, the SERC (Special Events Recreation Center), and the residence halls. These locations are all clearly marked on the campus map.

    Tips for Navigating Campus

    Here are some tips to help you navigate the Suny Brockport campus:

    • Take a campus tour or walk around campus to get familiar with the layout.
    • Use the campus map to plan your route ahead of time.
    • Pay attention to the building numbers to ensure you are going to the right place.
    • Don’t be afraid to ask for directions if you get lost.
    • Be aware of any construction or detours that may affect your route.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q: How big is the Suny Brockport campus?

    A: The Suny Brockport campus is approximately 464 acres.

    Q: How many buildings are on campus?

    A: There are over 50 buildings on the Suny Brockport campus.

    Q: Is there parking available on campus?

    A: Yes, there are several parking lots available on campus for students, faculty, and staff. Be sure to check the campus map for parking lot locations.

    Q: Are there any shuttle services available on campus?

    A: Yes, there are shuttle services available on campus to transport students to different locations on campus. Check the campus map for shuttle stop locations.

    Q: What should I do if I get lost on campus?

    A: Don’t be afraid to ask for directions! You can ask a fellow student, a faculty member, or stop by the information desk in the Seymour College Union for assistance.

    Conclusion

    Navigating the Suny Brockport campus can be overwhelming at first, but with the help of the campus map, you’ll be able to find your way around in no time. Remember to take your time, plan your route ahead of time, and don’t be afraid to ask for directions if you need help. Good luck!
