FIU South Campus Map: Navigating Your Way Around Campus Are you a new student at Florida International University (FIU) South Campus and feeling lost among the maze of buildings and roads? Don’t worry, we’ve got you covered! In this article, we’ll provide you with a comprehensive guide on how to navigate your way around the FIU South Campus map. Firstly, let’s take a look at the layout of the campus. The FIU South Campus is located in the heart of Miami, Florida, and spans over 200 acres. The campus consists of 8 schools and colleges, including the College of Arts, Sciences & Education, College of Engineering & Computing, and College of Business, among others. To make it easier for you to navigate the campus, FIU provides an interactive map on its official website. You can access this map by simply searching for “FIU South Campus Map” on Google or by visiting the FIU website. Once you have accessed the map, you’ll notice that it is color-coded and labeled with different buildings and facilities. The colors represent the various schools and colleges, making it easier for you to locate your desired destination. The map also includes important landmarks such as parking lots, shuttle stops, and dining options. Now, let’s move on to some frequently asked questions about the FIU South Campus map: Q. How do I navigate around the map? A. You can use the zoom function to get a better view of the map. Simply click on the “+” or “-” buttons to zoom in or out. You can also use the search bar to search for specific buildings or locations. Q. How do I find my classroom or office? A. You can use the building and room number to locate your classroom or office. The building numbers are labeled on the map, and the room numbers are displayed on the doors. Q. How do I get around the campus? A. FIU provides a shuttle service that runs throughout the campus. The shuttle stops are labeled on the map, and you can also track the shuttle in real-time using the “TransLoc” app. Q. What if I get lost on campus? A. Don’t panic! FIU provides a “Safe Ride” program that offers free transportation to and from any location on campus. You can call 305-348-7433 to request a safe ride. In conclusion, navigating your way around the FIU South Campus map can seem overwhelming at first, but with the help of this guide and the interactive map provided by FIU, you’ll be able to find your way around in no time. Remember to take advantage of the shuttle service and “Safe Ride” program if you ever need assistance. Good luck, and welcome to FIU South Campus!
