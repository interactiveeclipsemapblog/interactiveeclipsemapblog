    Introduction

    Howard University is a prestigious university located in the heart of Washington, D.C. It is a historically black university that has produced some of the most influential leaders in the world. The campus is vast and it can be overwhelming for new students and visitors. This guide will help you navigate the Howard University campus map.

    Getting Around

    The Howard University campus is spread over 256 acres and has more than 50 buildings. The campus map is your best friend when it comes to getting around. You can find the map on the Howard University website or pick up a physical copy at the welcome center. The map is color-coded and easy to read.

    How to Read the Map

    The Howard University campus map is divided into different sections. Each section is color-coded and corresponds to a specific part of the campus. The legend on the map will help you understand what each color means. You can find the legend in the bottom right-hand corner of the map.

    Important Buildings

    There are many important buildings on the Howard University campus. Here are some of the most important buildings:

    • Founders Library: The main library on campus
    • Blackburn University Center: The student center
    • Carnegie Hall: Home to the Department of Music
    • Andrew Rankin Memorial Chapel: A historic chapel on campus
    • Burr Gymnasium: Home to the Howard Bison basketball teams

    FAQs

    How do I get to Howard University?

    Howard University is located in the Shaw neighborhood of Washington, D.C. The best way to get to campus is by metro. The Shaw-Howard University metro station is located on the green and yellow lines.

    Where can I find parking on campus?

    There are several parking lots on campus. The closest parking lot to the welcome center is the Howard Center Parking Garage. You can also find parking near the Blackburn University Center.

    What should I do if I get lost on campus?

    If you get lost on campus, don’t worry. You can always ask for directions at the welcome center or any of the buildings on campus. You can also use the Howard University campus map to help you find your way.

    Conclusion

    Navigating the Howard University campus map can seem overwhelming at first, but with this guide, you’ll be able to find your way around campus like a pro. Remember to use the map, ask for directions when you need them, and explore all that Howard University has to offer.
