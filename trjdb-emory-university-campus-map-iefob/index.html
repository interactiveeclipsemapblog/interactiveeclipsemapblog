Emory University Campus Map: A Comprehensive Guide

admin June 15, 2022 No Comments

Welcome to a comprehensive guide to Emory University Campus Map in 2023! Emory University, located in Atlanta, Georgia, is a private research university with a history dating back to 1836. The university boasts an impressive campus spanning over 630 acres, with over 14,000 students and 2,500 faculty members. Navigating such a vast campus can be daunting, but fear not, as we have compiled all the necessary information to help you find your way around with ease.

Location and Overview

Emory University is located in the Druid Hills neighborhood of Atlanta, Georgia, and boasts a beautiful campus of over 630 acres. The campus is divided into four main areas: the academic quadrangle, the Clairmont campus, the Briarcliff campus, and the Oxford campus. The academic quadrangle is the heart of the university, housing most of the academic buildings, libraries, and administrative offices.

Getting Around

Emory University provides several transportation options for students, faculty, and staff, including shuttle buses, bike share programs, and carpool options. The university also has a comprehensive campus map available online and in print, which can be extremely helpful in navigating the campus.

Question:

How can I access the Emory University campus map?

Answer:

The campus map is available online and in print. You can access the online map by visiting the Emory University website, and the print version can be obtained from various locations on campus, including the welcome center and the library.

Academic Quadrangle

The academic quadrangle is the heart of the university, and it is where most of the academic buildings are located. These buildings include the Candler Library, the Woodruff Library, the Goizueta Business School, the Emory School of Medicine, and many others. The academic quadrangle is also home to several dining options and study spaces.

Question:

Can visitors access the academic buildings on the Emory University campus?

Answer:

Yes, visitors are allowed to access the academic buildings on the Emory University campus. However, some buildings may require a valid Emory ID to enter, so it is best to check with the specific building’s policies before attempting to enter.

Clairmont Campus

The Clairmont campus is located about two miles from the academic quadrangle and is home to several residence halls, dining options, and recreational facilities. The campus also houses the Emory Student Center, which is a hub for student activities and events.

Question:

Is there transportation available between the academic quadrangle and the Clairmont campus?

Answer:

Yes, Emory University provides shuttle buses that run between the academic quadrangle and the Clairmont campus at regular intervals throughout the day.

Briarcliff Campus

The Briarcliff campus is located about five miles from the academic quadrangle and is home to the Emory Healthcare system. The campus houses several hospitals, clinics, and research facilities.

Question:

Is there public transportation available to the Briarcliff campus?

Answer:

Yes, there are several public transportation options available to the Briarcliff campus, including MARTA buses and trains.

Oxford Campus

The Oxford campus is located about 40 miles east of Atlanta and is home to Emory’s liberal arts college. The campus houses several academic buildings, residence halls, and recreational facilities.

Question:

Is transportation available between the academic quadrangle and the Oxford campus?

Answer:

Yes, Emory University provides shuttle buses that run between the academic quadrangle and the Oxford campus at regular intervals throughout the day.

Campus Safety

Emory University is committed to providing a safe and secure campus environment for all students, faculty, and staff. The university has a comprehensive safety and security program, which includes emergency preparedness, crime prevention, and community policing.

Question:

How can I report a safety concern on the Emory University campus?

Answer:

Emory University provides several options for reporting safety concerns, including contacting the Emory Police Department, submitting a report online, or using the LiveSafe app.

In Conclusion

Emory University is a beautiful and vast campus, but with the help of the campus map and transportation options, navigating it can be a breeze. We hope this guide has provided you with all the necessary information to make your visit to Emory University a success. Happy exploring!
