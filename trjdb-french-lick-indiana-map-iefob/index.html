Tips for Exploring French Lick

Now that you know how to use the French Lick Indiana map, here are some tips to help you make the most of your trip:

3. Try Your Luck at the Casino

The French Lick Casino is a popular attraction for visitors. The casino features slot machines, table games, and a sportsbook. Even if you’re not a gambler, the casino is worth a visit for its historic architecture.
