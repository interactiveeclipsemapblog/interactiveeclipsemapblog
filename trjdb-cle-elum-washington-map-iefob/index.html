Introduction

Cle Elum is a small town located in Washington State, known for its beautiful natural surroundings and outdoor recreation opportunities. If you’re planning a trip to Cle Elum or just want to learn more about this charming town, then this guide is for you. In this article, we’ll explore the Cle Elum Washington map, highlighting the best places to visit, eat, and stay in town.

Where is Cle Elum Located?

Cle Elum is located in the eastern part of Washington State, about 80 miles east of Seattle. It’s situated in the Cascade Mountains and surrounded by beautiful forests, rivers, and lakes.

What to Do in Cle Elum

There are plenty of outdoor activities to enjoy in Cle Elum, including hiking, fishing, and skiing. The town is also home to a number of museums and galleries, as well as several restaurants and shops.

Hiking Trails

One of the best things about visiting Cle Elum is the hiking trails. There are several trails in the area, ranging from easy to difficult. Some of the most popular trails include the Iron Horse Trail, the Cle Elum River Trail, and the Coal Mines Trail.

Fishing Spots

Cle Elum is also a great destination for fishing enthusiasts. The Cle Elum River is one of the best places to fish for trout and steelhead, while Lake Cle Elum is a popular spot for fishing and boating.

Skiing and Snowboarding

During the winter months, Cle Elum is a popular destination for skiing and snowboarding. The town is located near several ski resorts, including Snoqualmie Pass and Stevens Pass.

Where to Eat in Cle Elum

There are several great restaurants in Cle Elum, offering a range of cuisine from American to Italian. Some of the most popular restaurants in town include The Cottage Cafe, the Red Bird Cafe, and the Cle Elum Bakery.

Where to Stay in Cle Elum

Cle Elum offers a variety of lodging options, including hotels, motels, and bed and breakfasts. Some of the most popular places to stay in town include the Suncadia Resort, the Cle Elum Lodge, and the Timber Lodge Inn.
