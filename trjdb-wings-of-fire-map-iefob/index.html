Introduction

If you are a fan of the popular book series Wings of Fire by Tui T. Sutherland, you might have wondered about the geography of the world where the story takes place. In this article, we will explore the Wings of Fire map, which shows the different continents, kingdoms, and landmarks in the world of Pyrrhia and Pantala.

What is Wings of Fire?

Wings of Fire is a young adult fantasy series that follows the adventures of five dragonets, Clay, Tsunami, Glory, Starflight, and Sunny, who are destined to end a long-standing war between the dragon tribes of Pyrrhia. Later in the series, the story expands to include the continent of Pantala, where a new queen and her allies fight against the evil HiveWings.

What is the Wings of Fire Map?

The Wings of Fire Map is an official map created by the author, Tui T. Sutherland, that depicts the different locations and kingdoms in the world of Pyrrhia and Pantala. The map was first published in the book The Dragonet Prophecy, which is the first book in the series.

The Map of Pyrrhia

The Wings of Fire Map shows that Pyrrhia is divided into seven different kingdoms, each ruled by a different dragon tribe. These kingdoms are: – The Mud Kingdom – The Sand Kingdom – The Sky Kingdom – The Sea Kingdom – The Rainforest Kingdom – The Ice Kingdom – The Night Kingdom Each kingdom has its own unique geography, climate, and culture. For example, the Mud Kingdom is a swampy region that is home to the MudWings, while the Ice Kingdom is a freezing wasteland inhabited by the IceWings.

The Map of Pantala
