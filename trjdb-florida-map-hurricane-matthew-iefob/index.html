What is Hurricane Matthew?

Hurricane Matthew was a Category 5 hurricane that hit Florida in 2016. It was one of the most powerful hurricanes to hit the state in over a decade, causing significant damage to homes, businesses, and infrastructure.

How Can You Prepare for Future Hurricanes?

There are several things that you can do to prepare for future hurricanes. First, you should make sure that you have a hurricane kit that includes food, water, and other supplies. Second, you should make sure that your home is prepared for a hurricane by securing windows and doors and trimming trees and bushes. Finally, you should stay informed about hurricane updates and evacuation orders.

What Should You Do During a Hurricane?

If a hurricane is approaching, it is important to follow all evacuation orders and seek shelter in a safe location. If you are unable to evacuate, you should stay indoors and away from windows and doors. You should also have a battery-powered radio or television to stay informed about the hurricane’s progress.

What Should You Do After a Hurricane?

After a hurricane has passed, it is important to take stock of any damage to your home or property. You should also stay informed about any road closures or power outages in your area. Finally, you should contact your insurance company to begin the process of filing a claim.
