Arkansas Power Outage Map: What You Need To Know

admin April 30, 2022 No Comments

The Importance of Knowing About Power Outages in Arkansas

If you live in Arkansas, it is important to know about power outages and how they can affect your daily life. Power outages can be caused by various factors such as severe weather conditions, equipment failure, or overloading of the power grid. By knowing about power outages, you can prepare yourself and your family for any emergencies that may arise.

What is an Arkansas Power Outage Map?

An Arkansas Power Outage Map is a tool that allows you to see where power outages are occurring in real-time. It provides information on the number of customers affected, the estimated time of restoration, and the cause of the outage. This map is especially useful during severe weather conditions when power outages are more likely to occur.

Where Can You Find the Arkansas Power Outage Map?

The Arkansas Power Outage Map can be found on the website of the Arkansas Electric Cooperative Corporation (AECC). You can access the map by visiting their website and clicking on the “Outage Map” tab. The AECC is responsible for providing electricity to more than 500,000 customers in Arkansas.

How to Use the Arkansas Power Outage Map?

To use the Arkansas Power Outage Map, simply zoom in on the map to see where power outages are occurring. You can also search for a specific address to see if there are any power outages in that area. The map will provide you with information on the estimated time of restoration, the cause of the outage, and the number of customers affected.

What to Do During a Power Outage?

During a power outage, it is important to stay calm and follow these tips:

  • Turn off all appliances and electronics to prevent damage from power surges when the power comes back on.
  • Use flashlights or battery-operated lanterns instead of candles to avoid fire hazards.
  • Keep refrigerator and freezer doors closed as much as possible to keep food from spoiling.
  • Do not use generators or grills indoors as they can produce dangerous carbon monoxide fumes.
  • Stay informed by listening to a battery-operated radio or checking the Arkansas Power Outage Map for updates.

How to Report a Power Outage?

If you experience a power outage, you can report it to your local utility company or the AECC. You can do this by calling their customer service hotline or using their online outage reporting tool. It is important to provide as much information as possible, such as your address and the cause of the outage, to help them restore power as quickly as possible.

Conclusion

Knowing about power outages and how to use the Arkansas Power Outage Map can help you prepare for emergencies and stay informed during severe weather conditions. Remember to stay safe and follow the tips provided during a power outage. If you experience a power outage, report it to your local utility company or the AECC to help them restore power as quickly as possible.
