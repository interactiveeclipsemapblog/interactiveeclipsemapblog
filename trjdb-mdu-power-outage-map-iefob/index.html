Introduction

In 2023, power outages are still a common occurrence in many parts of the world, and the situation is not any different for the customers of Montana-Dakota Utilities (MDU). However, with the introduction of the MDU power outage map, it has become easier for customers to stay informed and prepared during power outages. What is the MDU Power Outage Map?

The MDU power outage map is an online tool that allows customers to track power outages in real-time. The map provides up-to-date information on the locations and durations of outages, as well as estimated restoration times. The map is accessible through the MDU website or mobile app and is available to all MDU customers.

How to Access the MDU Power Outage Map

To access the MDU power outage map, simply visit the MDU website or download the MDU mobile app. Once you are on the website or app, look for the “Outage Map” tab or icon. Click on the tab or icon, and the map will appear on your screen.

Using the MDU Power Outage Map

The MDU power outage map is user-friendly and easy to use. The map displays outage information in real-time, including the number of outages, the locations of outages, and the estimated restoration times. The map also includes a legend that helps customers understand the different colors and symbols on the map.

How to Report a Power Outage

If you are experiencing a power outage and do not see it reflected on the MDU power outage map, you should report the outage to MDU as soon as possible. You can report an outage by calling MDU’s customer service hotline or by using the “Report an Outage” feature on the MDU website or app.

What to Do During a Power Outage

During a power outage, it is important to stay safe and prepared. If you have a generator, make sure it is in good working condition and that you have enough fuel to keep it running. Avoid using candles or other open flames for light, as they can be a fire hazard. Instead, use flashlights or battery-powered lanterns. Keep your refrigerator and freezer doors closed to keep your food cold for as long as possible.

The MDU power outage map is a valuable tool for MDU customers, especially during power outages. By providing real-time outage information, the map helps customers stay informed and prepared.
