Map of Texas Airports: Tips for Travelers in 2023 Are you planning a trip to Texas? If so, it’s important to know about the different airports in the state. The largest and most well-known is Dallas/Fort Worth International Airport, which is located between the cities of Dallas and Fort Worth.
