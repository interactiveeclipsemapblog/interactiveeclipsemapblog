Exploring The Bravely Second World Map

The World of Luxendarc

Bravely Second takes place in the world of Luxendarc, a realm of magic and adventure. The world map is expansive and filled with diverse environments, from snowy mountains to lush forests to vast deserts. As you progress through the game, you’ll unlock new areas and discover hidden secrets.

The Basics of Exploration

To navigate the world map, simply use the directional pad to move your character around. You’ll encounter enemies and treasure chests along the way, so be sure to keep your weapons and abilities at the ready. You can also use the map to plan out your route and find any hidden areas.

Quests and Side Missions

In addition to the main storyline, there are plenty of side missions and quests to complete. These can range from simple fetch quests to epic battles against powerful foes. Completing these missions will often earn you valuable rewards and experience points.

Unlocking New Areas

As you progress through the game, you’ll unlock new areas of the world map. These areas are often gated behind obstacles such as locked doors or impassable terrain. To unlock these areas, you’ll need to find specific items or complete certain tasks.

Collecting Summoning Gems

One way to unlock new areas is to collect summoning gems. These powerful artifacts can be found hidden throughout the world map. Once you have collected all the gems, you can use them to summon powerful creatures to aid you in battle.

Completing Events and Challenges

Another way to unlock new areas is to complete special events and challenges. These can range from simple puzzles to intense battles against powerful enemies. Be sure to keep an eye out for these events as you explore the world map.

The Importance of Exploration

Exploring the world map is essential to progressing through the game. Not only will you uncover new areas and items, but you’ll also gain valuable experience points and level up your characters. Don’t be afraid to take your time and thoroughly explore each area.
